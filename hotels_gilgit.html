Gilgit Serena Hotel



             




WELCOME TO GILGIT SERENA HOTEL


Classic comfort meets modern elegance in one of the most stunning settings in Gilgit-Baltistan. Gilgit Serena Hotel offers new experiences and discoveries amid an ancient setting. Where nature lovers explore the snow-capped beauty of the Karakoram mountain range, groups gather in refined intimacy, and all – business and leisure travellers alike – enjoy superlative local cuisine, heritage-style accommodations and personalised service in a safe, secure environment.
Gilgit Serena Hotel is more than an inviting gateway to the legendary Silk Road, far beyond a tranquil mountain sanctuary, more than simply a Pakistan vacation destination, and more than an inspiring setting to meet or celebrate. It is a place for reconnecting with nature, a springboard to adventure and an introduction to the vibrant culture and traditions and warm-hearted people of the Gilgit Valley.


Standard Room


Designed for business travellers and leisure guests seeking more space, the Standard Room features a king bed, a separate sitting area, private balcony with outdoor seating and an attached bathroom.


Dumani Restaurant


Renowned throughout Gilgit for its Pakistani, Continental and Chinese cuisine accompanied by views of the hotel’s lawns. On Sunday, a High Tea is accompanied by buffet and a la carte service. Breakfast is complimentary for all hotel guests.
