Abbottabad Local Transport


There are few choices in Abbottabad to travel arround the city. The local transport is good, efficient and cost saving beside comfort level. Suzuki is the fine option to travel, it has the large chain in the city which cover the most areas like ilyasi mosque, harnoi and shimla hills.Nowadays, suzuki costs 15rs/person for single ride. To travel across the whole city multiple suzukis changeover can do the the work, and it is the most used transport arround the city.

Another Choice is dabba, dabba is the second most transport in Abbottabad. People can use Dabba personally for their purposes and to go somewhere in the city althoug dabba can go Thandiani and Sajikot. Suzaki didn't go to Sajikot and Thandiani , other than that few other vehicles go there like 4 by 4 jeep and van with the condition of individual person cost.

Careem's access is available in Abbottabad, it's new in the corner but it works just fine in the city. so Careem is another choice, so choices are many but it depends upon your preferences.
