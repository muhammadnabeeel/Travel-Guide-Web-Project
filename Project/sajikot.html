Sajikot Waterfall


Sajikot waterfall also known as Sajikot Abshar is situated near Havelian Teshil of distt Abbottabad, If you are traveling form Main GT Road from Peshawar /Rawalpindi then just enter to Havelian city at Overhead bridge at entrance and in 30-40 min you reached in Sajikot bazaar then half an hour at your final destination and then follow the sign board after almost one hour form Havelian City entrance you reached Sajikot Waterfall.
