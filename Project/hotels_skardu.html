Baltistan Continental Hotel Skardu Pakistan



             




INTRODUCTION


Between the crossroads of beauty, nature and time, in the heart of the city lies the superb luxurious modern hotel facility, where you can feel at home, called “baltistan continental hotel”.

The four story building with 23 rooms, reflects the traditional balti architecture equipped with modern amenities like rent a car service & internet facility. The location of the hotel provides the spectacular view of kharpocho fort, traditional village, historical Buddha rock and lot more.


GUEST ROOMS


Each Guest room offers an effortless serenity inside that echoes the splendid views over the lush mountainous landscapes. Kharpocho fort, traditional village & peaks can be seen from the balcony of the room.


RESTAURANT


Restaurant provides a comfortable atmosphere where tradition, elegance and diversity combine with excellent service make every meal unforgettable. BCH offers a blend of mufti-cultural cuisines.
